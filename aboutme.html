About Me

Welcome to my page! I'm Elif Polat, a passionate and dedicated architecture student based in Istanbul, Turkey. My journey in the world of architecture began with a deep love for design and creativity. Born and raised in Istanbul, I have always been fascinated by the intricate blend of history and modernity that defines this vibrant city. After completing my primary education, I pursued my passion for the arts by studying ballet at the Mimar Sinan Fine Arts Conservatory. I continued my academic journey at Burak Bora Anatolian High School. Currently, I am a fourth-year architecture student at Mimar Sinan Fine Arts University, where I am continuously inspired by the rich architectural heritage of Istanbul. Alongside my studies, I also work part-time at CM Architecture Office. Outside of architecture, I enjoy exploring new cultures, traveling, and capturing moments through photography. Thank you for visiting my page, and I hope you enjoy learning more about my journey and passion for architecture.
